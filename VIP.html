Georgia Tech Vertically Integrated Project Program

Electronic ARTrium

Team Goal: Design and build an interesting and engaging interactive sensor-driven dynamic art installation. The installation consists of a combination of three kinds of original art — sound, mechatronics, and video — that all respond to participant motion in a synchronized way. Key points in participants’ poses, extracted in real time from camera output, are processed and then streamed over Ethernet to control the images, sound, and mechatronic movement. The goal is to give the participants a sense of wonder and intrigue as they experiment with how their body movements change the art.
